Welome!

Pi-Curious was born out the desire to learn and to dive into the Raspberry Pi. The Pi is incredibly powerful there's not mistaking that and what you can do with it is limited by your own imagination. Every day hundreds of new Pi projects are born. This can make getting into the Raspberry Pi very intimidating. Pi-Curious is here to help you get the things you want out of the Pi and hopefully inspire you to create something that is awesome and beneficial to you. Below are links to our events in Northeast Ohio, tutorials and handy tools to aid you in satisfying your Pi-Curiosity. Enjoy!
